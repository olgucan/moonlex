Our Story

MOONLEX is a company born from the idea and objective to develop a type of respirator with the maximum possible breathability and the highest bacterial efficiency to fight against the air pollution and SARS-CoV-2 (Coronavirus).

We work together to design, create and produce unique respirators that stand out: an essential product, in compliance with the highest standards in the World, that consumers can trust.

From the get go, a can-do attitude, speed and hard work, as well as a lot of enthusiasm have driven our founder, partners and supporters to create MOONLEX from scratch in these difficult times to face the new global challenges…

Because the work we’re doing now is better than the work we were doing six months ago. And the work we’ll be doing six months from now will be better than the work we’re doing today. Because we wake up every day with a sense to serve and facilitate so that others can protect themselves and experience more freedom and security around the World.

Our goal is to work with customers who believe what we believe so that we can all succeed in securing healthy and bright future. We’re looking for partners to stand shoulder-to-shoulder with us in pursuit of the same goal.

We offer you masks of the highest quality produced entirely in North Macedonia with all the guarantees and the most prestigious certificates in Europe. NB CE 2890, CIS INSTITUT.

That is why our materials meet the highest quality standards on the market.

We guarantee a constant production and delivery in record time.

We Care About Our Planet

“How you treat the one reveals how you regard the many, because everyone is ultimately A ONE.” – Steven R. Covey

At MOONLEX we love our planet. As one of our founding principles, we want to build a business that is climate neutral from day one.

Starting a business, climate neutrality is only one of many challenges. For most suppliers this is still new territory, and doing things the old (polluting) way is much easier, and sometimes cheaper. The lack of transparency along the value chain makes it even harder to accomplish our believes we have committed to at MOONLEX:

We are living in a period with record temperatures, water shortages leading to crop failures, deforestation, species extinction, weather extremes, and climate refugees displaced by drought and flood. This cannot go on – we must set a course for living in harmony within the boundaries of nature.

We believe that the world has to be and also can be completely powered by renewable energies by 2050 – and in some regions as soon as 2030 – and that this has to be done on the basis of a circular economy, in which there will be reusable resources, shared goods, and hardly any waste. Our planet is rich with abundant nature, clean water, and vital resources, all of which are essential for the survival of future generations.

When you buy MOONLEX face mask, you are automatically contributing to create a market that delivers safe quality products produced in the most environmentally responsible way possible.

Europe is (in)famous for its regulations, norms and rules. We turn this into our advantage. Analysing the market situation during the Covid pandemic and the regulatory landscape for face masks, we realised that a major part of the supply did not comply with EU standards.

Quality and safety regulations are there to protect consumers and ensure verifiable product quality and traceability. We worked hard to comply with every norm applicable. Made in Europe is a symbol of the highest quality.

All MOONLEX products are 100% manufactured in Europe. Our packaging is sourced from suppliers in various European countries, in respect with our goal towards Climate Pledge wherever possible.

Light, comfort & design

MOONLEX Respirators are made from light and comfortable material and world-class water electret meltblown. We go to great lengths to provide our customers with the cosiest and most breathable respirator available on the market today. We have achieved significant and long-term antimicrobial properties highly effective in the fight against microorganisms (e.g. SARS-CoV-2).

FFP2 & FFP3 certification

MOONLEX Respirator meets European norm EN 149:2001 + A1:2009 and has FFP2 & FFP3 certification. MOONLEX Respirators have passed the tests of wide recognized testing institutions, such as CIS INSTITUT. VFE and BFE test result of particle blocking is ≥ 99%. MOONLEX Respirators have world class filtrating technology with multi-layer hypoallergenic composition without adhesives. All our protective teams are graphene free. The technology is certified by NB CE 2890 (SARS-CoV-2, H3N2, H1N1).
