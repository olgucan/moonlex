What We Offer

Avoid spread by aerosols

MOONLEX filtering face piece respirators can be used for respiratory protection against various airborne contaminants. It is extremely effective (94-99,9%) against tiny particulate matter of less than 0.3 and 2.5 microns in diameter (PM0.3 and PM2.5 respectively) among which are many infectious agents. For the purpose of protecting yourself from influenza viruses (SARS, avian flu, coronavirus, etc.), respirator masks were recommended as part of the PPE by the World Health Organization (WHO).

5 Protective layers

MOONLEX mask is composed of 5 hydrophobic layers of high-quality non-woven breathable fabric and electret meltblown filters that ensure a filtration ofâ‰¥ 99% against particulate aerosols.

Protection against solids, water-based aerosols and oil-based aerosols, reducing small particles inhaled and exhaled by the wearer.

High breathability

MOONLEX masks stand out for their high breathability. Its design means that the mask does not stick to the face, providing a greater volume of air and therefore better breathability.

It will be particularly appreciated by the elderly, athletes and anyone who uses a respirator for a long time in the exercise of his profession.

Packaging

MOONLEX mask is individually packaged in a bag made up mostly of cellulosic material (kraft type). Once the mask has been used, it can be stored in the bag again which, with its holes, facilitates the hygiene and integrity of the product.

Each box can include 5, 10, 25, 50 or 100 individualized masks packed separately to guarantee that it is 100% safe and hygienic, avoiding contamination of the mask before use.
